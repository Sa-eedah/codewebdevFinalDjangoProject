FAQ ‐ Orders

When will I be charged for my order?

When your order is placed, the total amount of the order will be reserved from the account you used. This amount will only be deducted from your account when the order has been processed in store. You will receive a confirmation email when the order is placed, and again when the order has been processed.

What can I order online?

You can order from the full range of products on our online store available for your delivery area.

What happens if my order is damaged or I am unsatisfied?

If your order is damaged on delivery, please retain the insert that comes with your order and return your items at your nearest Shop.Co store.

How can I track my order?

You will receive three emails from us when you place an order on our website or app.

My order has not arrived when I expected it to, What can I do?

Please contact us on shop.co@gmail.com and we will investigate your claim. Remember to include your order number.

How do I cancel an order?

Allow us to help with this, Please contact us on shop.co@gmail.com. Remember to include your order number.
